## JANUARY
**Performance:** Strong start ($1.94M).
**Context:**
- **Post-Holiday Momentum:** Capitalized on gift card redemptions and "New Year, New You" trends, driving sales in 'Health & Wellness' and 'Fitness'.
- **Clearance:** Successful liquidation of 2022 inventory in 'Clothing & Fashion' cleared warehouse space.

## FEBRUARY
**Performance:** Significant drop to $1.46M (-24%).
**Context:**
- **Weather Event:** "Winter Storm Uriah" shut down logistics hubs in the North/West for 5 days.
- **Supply Chain:** "Lunar New Year" factory closures in Asia extended longer than anticipated, causing stockouts in high-margin 'Electronics'.

## MARCH
**Performance:** Recovery to $1.77M.
**Context:**
- **Restocking:** Inventory from Asia finally arrived, fulfilling backorders from February.
- **Marketing:** The "Spring Forward" sale successfully targeted early adopters of outdoor furniture.

## APRIL
**Performance:** Slight dip to $1.68M.
**Context:**
- **Tax Season:** Consumer discretionary spending historically tightens in the US during tax filing weeks (April 1-15).
- **Category Shift:** Shift from indoor to outdoor categories caused a temporary lull as 'Home Decor' slowed down before 'Garden' picked up.

## MAY
**Performance:** Steady growth to $1.75M.
**Context:**
- **Campaign:** "Spring Home Refresh" campaign launched.
- **Smart Home:** Aggressive discounting on Smart Plugs drove volume but lowered average order value (AOV).

## JUNE
**Performance:** Drop to $1.60M.
**Context:**
- **Pre-Summer Lull:** Typical seasonal trough as consumers save for summer vacations.
- **Competitor Action:** A major competitor launched a surprise mid-year sale, eroding our market share in 'Toys & Games'.

## JULY
**Performance:** Summer peak at $1.87M.
**Context:**
- **Prime Season:** High demand for 'Travel & Luggage' and 'Summer Fashion'.
- **Heatwave:** Record temperatures drove unseasonal demand for cooling appliances (fans, AC units) in the 'Home Appliances' sector.

## AUGUST
**Performance:** Decline to $1.64M.
**Context:**
- **Macroeconomics:** Inflation reports released early August dampened consumer sentiment.
- **Back-to-School:** While 'Books & Stationery' performed well, it couldn't offset the sharp decline in 'Leisure' and 'Entertainment' spending.

## SEPTEMBER
**Performance:** Yearly low at $1.55M.
**Context:**
- **Logistics Crisis:** A two-week labor strike at West Coast ports stranded containers. 'Gaming' and 'Sports' inventory was stuck at sea.
- **Stockouts:** Key Q4 items did not arrive on time, leading to missed revenue opportunities.

## OCTOBER
**Performance:** Record month at $1.94M.
**Context:**
- **Product Launch:** Global launch of the "Alpha Series" electronics line. Pre-orders fulfilled this month.
- **Early Holiday:** Amazon's "Prime Day" in October created a "halo effect," boosting online shopping traffic across the board.

## NOVEMBER
**Performance:** Solid at $1.68M.
**Context:**
- **Calendar Shift:** Thanksgiving fell late, pushing the full impact of Cyber Monday billing into December for some accounts.
- **Waiting Game:** Consumers delayed purchases in weeks 1-3, waiting for Black Friday deals.

## DECEMBER
**Performance:** Strong finish at $1.85M.
**Context:**
- **Gift Rush:** 'Health & Wellness' kits became the viral gift of the season.
- **Shipping Deadlines:** A new partnership with a localized courier service allowed us to extend the "guaranteed delivery" window by 2 days, capturing late shoppers.
